Prabhāsa draws on three lines of work: data-efficient pretraining, morphology-aware masking, and computational treatments of the Pāṇinian and Navya-Nyāya traditions. The BabyLM challenge~\cite{Warstadt2023, Hu2024BabyLM} isolates training methodology from data scale through fixed 10M- and 100M-token budgets, evaluating on compositional tasks (SCAN, COGS, CFQ, BLiMP) and GLUE; competitive submissions such as LTG-BERT~\cite{Samuel2023LTG}, ELC-BERT~\cite{CharpentierSamuel2023}, and the 2024 winner GPT-BERT~\cite{CharpentierSamuel2024} encode structural priors in masking, architecture, or objective, with the official baselines (Strict 74.53, Strict-Small 65.08 BLiMP) as reference points. Morphology-aware masking conditions the masking distribution on linguistic structure (morpheme boundaries, syntactic depth, or semantic roles) rather than uniform randomness, as in curriculum approaches~\cite{Xu2020Curriculum} and morphology-aware modelling~\cite{Belinkov2017Morphology, Botha2014Morphology}.

The Pāṇinian framework~\cite{KiparskyStaal1969, Cardona1974Karaka}, a formal grammar from circa 500 BCE defining Sanskrit word formation and argument structure through kāraka roles, is implemented computationally via the Vidyut toolkit~\cite{Prasad2024Vidyut}; the Paribhāṣā interpretive meta-rules~\cite{Kielhorn1868Paribhasa} motivate our adaptive masking, which likewise applies meta-rules (morpheme- and role-aware) to govern an otherwise uniform operation. Classical Indian logic (Navya-Nyāya) supplies the theory of śābdabodha and Gadādhara's Vyutpattivāda, as analysed by Matilal~\cite{Matilal1990WordWorld} and Ganeri~\cite{Ganeri1999SemanticPowers}, which account for how sentence meaning is derived compositionally and motivate the role-prediction auxiliary. Appendix~\ref{app:repro} gives the full survey, including Sanskrit computational resources~\cite{Kulkarni2010Parser, Huet2003SanskritHeritage}. The novelty is the integration: kāraka-aware masking and morpheme embeddings tested under pre-registered, matched-budget, multi-seed protocols at the 10M- and 100M-token budgets, against the official leaderboard suite.
